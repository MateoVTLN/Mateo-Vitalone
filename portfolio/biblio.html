About

My academic and technical interests lie at the intersection of acoustics, signal processing, electroacoustics and sound engineering. My work focuses on understanding and designing systems that generate, shape and analyze sound, ranging from musical instruments to complex acoustic environments.


My interest in audio engineering originated from a passion for electric guitars and rock music. Curiosity about how instruments produce and shape sound gradually led me toward electronics, signal processing and acoustic engineering.

Academic Path

2017 – 2020

Musical Training – Electric Guitar aside Scientific Highschool program

Studied electric guitar and live performance at Muzik-Adicted music school. Regular stage performances helped develop musical creativity and experience in live sound environments. Highschool studies focused on scientific subjects, such as Physics and Engineering Sciences, providing a strong foundation for later academic pursuits.

2018 – Present

Electronics and Instrument Modification

Early experimentation with guitar electronics sparked an interest in circuit design, soldering and audio system modification. Projects included pickup rewiring, tone circuit modifications and small electronic modules for musical equipment.

2020 – 2023

Bachelor in Acoustics and Vibrations

Completed 2 years of a Bachelor's degree in General Physics and Chemistry at Jean François Champollion Université, Albi (FRANCE). Then specialized and completed a Bachelor's degree in Acoustics and Vibrations at Le-Mans Université, Le-Mans (FRANCE). The program provided a strong foundation in:

  • Wave physics and acoustics
  • Structural vibrations
  • Signal processing
  • Electroacoustics
  • Room acoustics
  • Measurement techniques and Metrology

2024 – Present

MSc Music and Acoustic Engineering

Currently pursuing the Master's degree in Music and Acoustic Engineering (specialization in Acoustic Engineering) at Politecnico di Milano, Milano (Italy). Academic work focuses on:

  • Audio signal processing
  • Machine learning for Audio
  • Electroacoustic systems
  • Acoustic modelling and vibration analysis
  • Music information retrieval

Research Interests

  • Medical Acoustics : Speech Signals and Breathing Physiology
  • Audio signal processing and machine learning
  • Acoustic modelling and vibration analysis
  • Music information retrieval
  • Vintage audio equipment and analog electronics
